# 05 — New Ascendancies: Spirit Walker & Martial Artist

Two new ascendancies arrive in 0.5. Both are well-supported by the new rune system and are considered strong league starters per pre-launch coverage.

**Caveat:** ascendancy node names, exact values, and "best builds" change rapidly in early league. The names below are from official reveal materials and pre-launch coverage (Maxroll, Mobalytics, Sportskeeda, Boostmatch). Verify exact values with `poe2_db_lookup` for the specific node, e.g. `term="Hollow_Focus_I"`.

---

## Spirit Walker (Huntress ascendancy)

**Identity:** Beast tamer + spirit channeler. Uses **Azmerian Wisps** (Stag, Owl, Bear) and tames **Unique Beast bosses** to fight alongside.

**Spanish name:** Caminante Espiritual / Caminante de los Espíritus.

### Three Wisps, three flavors

Each Wisp provides different benefits. Multiple can be active simultaneously — and using all three together unlocks a bonus node.

#### Stag (Vivid Wisp)
- **Vivid Stampede:** Movement-based skill. Every 20 meters traveled grants 1 Vivid Wisp stack (max 3). Attacking consumes all stacks and summons a torrent of Stag Spirits that charge across the area, releasing damaging shockwaves on impact.
- **The Morrigan's Guidance** (notable): Stags deal more damage and apply more Shock Magnitude per leap.
- Best for: long-corridor map clears, mobility-focused playstyle.

#### Owl (Primal Wisp)
- **Primal Bounty:** Causes Empowered Projectile Skills to fire **more projectiles with increased Projectile Speed**.
- **The Mhacha's Gift** (notable): further strengthens Owl bonuses.
- Best for: projectile-stacking builds (bow, crossbow, projectile spells).

#### Bear (Wild Wisp)
- **Wild Protector:** Summons a Bear Spirit as a **second Companion**.
- Best for: companion-focused builds.

### Tame Beast — the big new mechanic

- **The Natural Order** (notable): Grants `Tame Beast`. Lets you tame **Unique Beast bosses** as Companions.
- Pre-launch confirmed-tameable bosses (Spanish coverage): **Mighty Silverfist**, **Chimeral Beast**, **Rakkar**, **Icefang Claw**.
- Tamed bosses join you in combat as permanent companions.

### Sacred Unity (capstone-ish)

- Allocating **Wild Protector + Vivid Stampede + Primal Bounty** unlocks **Sacred Unity** as a bonus ascendancy node (free to allocate).
- Sacred Unity empowers all primal spirits further: damage and defense options.

### The Catha's Balance

- Makes your **Main Hand Weapon damage** scale damage of your Companions.
- Important: turns weapon investment into companion investment.

### Idolatry support

- Spirit Walker can use **Idolatry** (idol-socketed gear) for massive bonuses to companion-centric builds.
- Idol slots stack with Wisp bonuses.

### Key supporting unique

- **Sylvan's Effigy** (Stoic Sceptre, **new in 0.5**): Removes the Companion limit. Companions deal **85% more damage to targets you've Marked.** **Only drops from the Ritual pinnacle boss** — not from Orb of Chance. Expect to trade for one rather than self-find early.

### Pre-launch impressions

- **Strong league starter** — well-supported by the new rune system.
- Companion meta is reportedly "broken" by Sylvan's Effigy + Wild Protector + The Catha's Balance.

## Martial Artist (Monk ascendancy)

**Identity:** Master of illusions and "Hollow techniques." Summons spectral bells that explode on hit, channels clones that mimic attacks, and can convert gloves into weapons.

**Spanish name:** Artista Marcial.

**Pitched as:** the third Monk ascendancy alongside Invoker (elemental) and Acolyte of Chayula (chaos). Martial Artist leans **into your face at high velocity** — melee burst.

### Seven Hollow techniques, master three

The lore says Martial Artist "mastered three of the seven Hollow techniques. The other four remain unmastered." Mechanically, the ascendancy has multiple notables; you allocate a subset.

### Hollow Focus

- Automatically summons **ghostly Bells around you at intervals (within ~3m radius)**.
- Bells **resonate (deal AoE damage) when struck**.
- Hits on bells are **always Critical Hits**.
- Bells are **always Primed for Stun**.

### Hollow Resonance

- Attaches a **permanent astral Bell to your back**.
- Bell resonates and **creates a Shockwave** when you deal a Critical Hit with **any Attack Skill**.
- Effectively passive AoE damage on every crit.

### Hollow Form

- Channeled skill (metagem). Creates **multiple temporary illusions** of the Monk around you.
- Illusions perform the **socketed Attack** with **70% base Attack Speed and 80% Damage**.
- Auto-target the closest enemy if any are in range.
- Illusions disappear after the channel ends or after performing their attack.

### Way of the Stonefist

- Transforms gloves: grants **Evasion and Energy Shield per character level** on gloves, plus upgraded modifiers.
- Synergizes hard with **Hollow Palm Technique** (the regular passive that turns bare fists into weapons).
- Often the **first ascendancy node to take** for Hollow Palm builds — solves the glove slot defensively and offensively at the same time.

### Way of the Mountain

- Passive combat bonus (full text varies by source; verify with `poe2_db_lookup term="Way_of_the_Mountain_I"`).

### Runic Meridians

- Opens **additional Rune sockets** across your equipment. **Highly synergistic with the Runes of Aldur league.**
- Strong pick at the 4th ascendancy point if you have invested in runes.

### Build archetypes (pre-launch)

1. **Hollow Palm Bellshatter** — Hollow Palm + Boneshatter + Hollow Focus + Hollow Resonance. Boneshatter hits Hollow Focus bell (which is Primed for Stun and always-Crit), creating triple Shockwaves. Hollow Resonance's back-bell rings on each Crit, adding more Shockwaves.
2. **Quarterstaff Crit Monk** — Hollow Resonance + Way of the Stonefist + standard crit scaling. Reliable map clear with the back-bell triggering on every crit attack.
3. **Flicker Strike Clone Clearer** — Flicker Strike + Hollow Form + Hollow Resonance. Clones mimic your teleport-attacks, sweeping packs. Critical hits ring the bells.

### Pre-launch tier

- **Strong league starter.** Pre-patch tier lists rank Martial Artist above traditional Invoker for league start due to Runic Meridians solving league synergy, Way of the Stonefist solving the glove slot, and Hollow Palm being "a monster" through the campaign.
- Some interactions are **not fully understood pre-launch** (e.g. exact Shockwave-stacking math with Hollow Focus + Hollow Resonance + Boneshatter) — flag as `[Unverified]` until ladder confirms.

## Quick comparison

| | Spirit Walker | Martial Artist |
|---|---|---|
| Base class | Huntress | Monk |
| Playstyle | Companion + Wisp empowerment | Melee burst + illusions + bells |
| Primary scaling | Companion damage (via Weapon for The Catha's Balance), Wisp synergies | Crit (Hollow Resonance triggers), unarmed/Hollow Palm |
| League synergy | Wisp + Idol systems | Runic Meridians (extra rune sockets) |
| Headline mechanic | Tame Beast (unique boss companions) | Hollow Focus (always-crit bells) |
| Iconic unique | Sylvan's Effigy (Stoic Sceptre, drops from Ritual pinnacle) | — (Hollow Palm passive on regular tree, not unique-gated) |
| League start tier (pre-launch) | High | High |
